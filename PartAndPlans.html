EVE X TotallyRealMedicine "Innovation for All" plan and fund.

EVE has partnered with TotallyRealMedicine in producing medicine around the globe. The focus is currently shifted to producing a COVID-19 vaccine in which EVE has funded along with other companies. EVE’s goals include lowering global prices for ALL medicine, and helping end the COVID-19 pandemic.
